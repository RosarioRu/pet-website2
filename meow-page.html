Meow-Meow

Meow-Meow was a lonely creature, orphaned and living in the Portland Ikea until one day, a little boy decided he wanted to take him home. He has since become the boy's best friend, and resides happily in Portland, OR.

Meow-Meow's Favorite Things

  • Baby Emilio

  • Naps and cuddles
  • Eating imaginary mice
  • Screeching loudly

Things Meow-Meow Dislikes

  • Going in the washing machine
  • Loud scary dogs
  • Buho, Emilio's second favorite toy
  • Certain foods:
    • Potatoes
    • Apples
